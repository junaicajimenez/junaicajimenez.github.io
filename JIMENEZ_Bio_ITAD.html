Story of My Life

Childhood Years

My name is Junaica Jimenez. I was born on June 07, 2001 at St. Patrick Hospital Medical Center in Batangas City. 1 My mother is from Batangas City and my father is from Ilagan City, Isabela.2 I only have one sibling and our age gap is 10 years.3 I grew up in Quezon, Province because my family choose to stay here.4 We are staying at Quezon, Province up until now.5 When I have class and when my brother works, we are staying in Manila.6 I remember when was little, I have a big doll as tall as me, but I was suddenly got scared of dolls.7 One night I have this nightmare about dolls that I got scared and I tell them to throw it away.8 I ended up playing cars and guns because I really cannot stand seeing dolls.9 I remember in my childhood days I usually go to my cousin’s house to play.10 I always go with my aunt whenever she goes to my cousin’s house.11 I only have one cousin in our location because usually my cousins are from Batangas and Ilagan City.12 I used to watch cartoons every day and play with my toys.13 I don’t really have friends when I was young that I only have one cousin to play with.14 I also remember my 7th birthday when I requested that I want it to celebrate it in the beach.15 My parents grant it, and we celebrate it in the beach with other family relatives.16 When I start schooling, my uncle usually accompanies me and pick me up when it is time to go home.17 When I want something like toys, I usually ask my uncle to buy it for me.18 He spoiled me that whenever I want something, he will give it to me as long as he can.19 My father is far from us because he is working in Armed Forces and my mother is busy in her store, so I end up with my uncle every day.20

Teenage Years

I don’t usually go out because I don’t really have a friend in our barangay.1 In school I got friends because me and my classmate see each other every day.2 I learn to how play indoor and outdoor games because of my classmates and friends.3 Every day if we have a break or if we don’t have a teacher, we usually play outdoor and indoor games.4 The outdoor games that we are playing is badminton, volleyball, baseball and dodgeball.5 The indoor games that we are playing are jackstone, chess and we also play mobile games.6 I enjoyed my teenager years because of my classmate who became my friends.7 I also enjoy it because every year when it is time of fiesta, we will go to carnival and to have a good time.8 When we are about to graduate, I feel sad because this is the time that we will split up that each of us will go to other school.9 When it is time of summer, I used to go to Batangas for a vacation.10 My cousins taught me how to play chess and card games.11 There are so many mango trees in there so every afternoon we take mangoes.12 We dip our mangoes in soy sauce with sugar, it is really delicious that I really enjoyed it.13 My cousins are fond on traveling, road trip and going to beaches so when we are not busy, we usually go out.14 Every birthday of my mother’s father we always celebrate it by going to beaches or private pool.15 I am happy because we are complete that everyone of us in our family clan is there when we are celebrating it.16 I also learn how to drive motorcycle and it is my cousin’s husband who taught me.17 Every month when my cousin’s husband come home, he always teaches me how to drive motorcycle.18 I also remember that these years is good to celebrate Christmas.19 You can feel the presence of the Christmas season.20

College Years

I become distant to other people, I just only hang out with my closest friends.1 When I have some free time I usually go out with my other friends.2 When my friends have problem or if I have a problem, we usually meet and talk about our problem.3 I enjoyed this college year because of my friends whom I always with.4 Some of my friend introduce me Kpop, at first, I didn’t like it but, in the end, I enjoy listening to it.5 My classmates also fan of Kpop that we are playing some music when we don’t have a teacher and some of them are dancing.6 When it is semestral break, I go to Batangas to visit there and my cousin taught me how to drive a car.7 In the nearly end of the first semester in my first year, this is the time that I have many things in my head because my father got sick.8 In my second semester in my first year, I feel distracted because of my father illness.9 Every week and when we are not busy, me and my brother visiting him in the hospital.10 In the nearly end of the second semester, I fell down because my father died.11 When my friends know it, they visit me immediately and accompanied me.12 Whenever I think my father, I am feeling lonely and I end up crying.13 This pandemic helps us to ease our pain because are at home together.14 Now, when I am thinking about my father I am no longer crying.15 I am just thinking that my father is now happy, and he is now free from pain.16 It is now a year and I know we are in our halfway to fully accept that my father is no longer with us.17 This pandemic may be not good with the other people but for me it helps a lot in other way.18 This pandemic really helps us to cope in that circumstance.19 I am thankful that we are now good and I always praying that this pandemic will be over.20
