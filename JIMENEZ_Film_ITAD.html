TV shows


Stranger Things

This is an American science fiction horror series created by the Duffer Brothers
and the first season was released on July 15, 2016 in Netflix.

Its Okay To Not Be Okay

It is a South Korean romance drama tv series,
written by Jo Young and directed by Park Shin-woo.
It was broadcasted from June 20, 2020 to August 9, 2020 on tvN and Netflix.
The story is all about a man is employee in a psychiatric ward and a woman with an antisocial disorder,
who is popular writer of children’s books.


The Uncanny Counter

It is a South Korean series, and it is bass on the Daum webtoon Amazing Rumor by Jang Yi.
It was releases last November 28, 2020 up to January 24, 2021,
it is aired every Saturday and Sunday at 12 am in the Philippine time in Netflix.
The story is all about the hunting and fighting against with the evil spirits that scape for the afterlife to prey on human.




Movies


The Call

It is a South Korean thriller film directed by Lee Chung-hyun,
and it was releases last November 27, 2020.
The story is all about the two women from different time
who just connect through phone call which interchanges their fates.


IT

It is an American supernatural horror film based on Stephen King’s 1986 novel.
It has 2 chapter, the first chapter was released on September 7, 2017 and the chapter 2 was on August 26, 2019.
This movie is all about the experience of the group of friends as they are terrorizing by an evil entity (Pennywise)
that exploits the fears of its victims to disguise itself while hunting its prey,
then the friends must overcome their own personal fears to battle the murderous, bloodthirsty clown as Pennywise.


Shes Dating The Gangster

It is a Filipino movie, and it was released on July 16, 2014.
It is an age romantic comedy drama film that is based on the Pop Fiction book and
it was originally published on CandyMag.com Teen Talk section and became popular on the Wattpad platform.
