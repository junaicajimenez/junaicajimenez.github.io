What is health risk?




Health risk

A health risk is a possibility or chance wherein our health will be on harm or it will cause negative effects to your health. For example, people with obesity have a health risk of having diabetes, cigarette smokers have a high chance of having respiratory diseases like tuberculosis, lung cancer etc. We cannot mitigate a disease easily, but we can be safer if we take a test and have a prevention as the saying goes "Prevention is better than cure" because we don't know when we will die so that is why we must take care of ourselves.

Screening tests are important specially on women because it can help detect early problems before worse disease may happen. Women are more prone to breast cancers so that is why some others are already taking exams to detect any abnormalities before any symptoms are apparent.
